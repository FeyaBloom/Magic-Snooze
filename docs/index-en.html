How It Works

Simple, kind, and adapted to your pace

Four steps to build sustainable habits without stress.

  1. 1

    Set up your routines

    Create morning and evening routines. The app suggests gentle steps, but you choose what makes sense for you.

  2. 2

    Celebrate every tiny win

    Drank water? Went outside? Every small thing deserves celebration with confetti 🎊.

  3. 3

    Clear your mind

    Tasks, thoughts, ideas — drop them here. You don't have to keep everything in your head anymore.

  4. 4

    Press Pause when needed

    Hard day? Activate a Rest Day. Your streak and stats stay intact.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Tools designed for how your brain works, not against it.

1

Protection against burnout

The main reason people quit productivity apps. Magic Snooze solves it in three ways.

🎉

Tiny Victories

8 micro-celebrations with confetti for when you need a bit of light in your day.

💤

Rest Days

Press Pause to stop one day of progress without breaking your streak or affecting your stats.

🧊

Weekly Freeze

If you miss a day without planning it, the app automatically absorbs it once per week. Freeze resets every Monday.

2

Structure for daily life

The tools you'll use every day. Simple, visual, and low-noise.

⏰

Custom Routines

Morning and evening routines with visual progress bars. They reset automatically at midnight.

✅

Tasks Without Overload

Add one-time tasks, check off what you did, and free mental space. Clean, direct view to reduce executive block.

📝

Quick Notes

Capture ideas with text, optional image attachments, and voice notes. A simple place so important thoughts don't get lost.

3

Progress without pressure

Celebrate what you've achieved, not what you missed.

🎢

Positive Stats

See only your wins and progress. The app highlights positive moments to keep you motivated.

🌷

Victory Garden

A visual garden that blooms with every tiny victory you celebrate. Watch your progress grow in a tangible, beautiful way.

🔥

Smart Streak System

Track current and best streak. The app protects and encourages you, it doesn't punish you.

🌟

Levels & Achievements

Earn levels (Novice → Hero) and unlock 8 achievements based on your monthly engagement.

🎨

Dynamic Themes

⛅ Daydream and 🌙 Night Forest. Auto-switch at 7:00 and 19:00, or manual.

🔒

Total Privacy

All data stays on your device. No signup, no tracking, no servers.

🌍

Catalan and More

Available in Catalan, English, Spanish, and Russian. Automatic language detection on first launch.

🔔

Gentle Reminders

Kind and optional notifications. No aggressive messaging. You're fully in control.
